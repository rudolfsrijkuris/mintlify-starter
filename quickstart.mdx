title: "Quickstart"
description: "From sign-up to your first published post in minutes."
---

## 1. Create an account

Go to [postezi.com](https://postezi.com) and click **Get Postezi**. Enter your email and click the magic link — no password needed.

## 2. Purchase lifetime access

After signing in you'll land on the billing page. Postezi is a one-time $99 lifetime deal — no subscriptions. Complete the purchase to unlock the dashboard.

## 3. Add your Anthropic API key

Go to **Settings → Anthropic API Key**. Paste your key from [console.anthropic.com](https://console.anthropic.com). This is required for GitHub → post transforms. Your key is encrypted and never shared.

<Tip>
  Postezi uses your own Anthropic key (BYOK) so you're always in control of usage and costs.
</Tip>

## 4. Connect a social account

Go to **Accounts** and connect at least one platform:

- **Bluesky** — enter your handle and an [app password](https://bsky.app/settings/app-passwords)
- **LinkedIn** — OAuth flow, redirects back automatically
- **X / Twitter** — OAuth flow, redirects back automatically
- **Threads** — OAuth flow, redirects back automatically

## 5. Connect a GitHub repository

Go to **Sources** and connect your GitHub account, then select a repository. Postezi installs a webhook and listens for pushes, merged PRs, and releases.

When a qualifying event fires, Postezi uses Claude to draft posts for each connected platform and adds them to your queue for review.

## 6. Review and publish

Go to **Posts → Queue** to see AI-drafted posts. You can edit, approve, schedule, or discard each one. Approved posts publish immediately; scheduled posts go out at the time you pick.

You can also write posts manually from **Compose**.
