Postezi is a social media publishing dashboard built for developers and indie founders. Connect your platforms once, then publish or schedule posts to Bluesky, LinkedIn, and X from the dashboard or automatically from GitHub.

## What you can do

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Dashboard" icon="layout-dashboard" href="/dashboard/compose">
    Compose and schedule posts, review AI-drafted content, and connect your social accounts.
  </Card>
  <Card title="GitHub Sources" icon="github" href="/dashboard/sources">
    Connect a repo and Postezi auto-drafts social posts from your commits, PRs, and releases.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
